Operations Optimization Initiative

Timeline 6 Months
Role Strategy Lead
Team Size 12 People
Process Improvement Change Management Data Analysis Cross-functional Leadership

Overview

Led a transformative company-wide initiative to streamline operational processes, resulting in significant efficiency gains and improved cross-team collaboration. This project touched every department and required careful stakeholder management and change management strategies.

The Challenge

Our company was experiencing growing pains as we scaled. Legacy processes that worked for a team of 20 were breaking down with a team of 100+. Key challenges included:

  • Redundant workflows across departments
  • Lack of standardized processes
  • Poor visibility into operational metrics
  • Siloed teams with minimal cross-functional collaboration
  • Manual data entry consuming 15+ hours per week per team

Approach & Strategy

I developed a three-phase approach to tackle this systematically:

Phase 1: Discovery & Analysis (2 months)

  • Conducted 25+ stakeholder interviews across all departments
  • Mapped existing processes and identified pain points
  • Analyzed operational data to quantify inefficiencies
  • Benchmarked against industry best practices

Phase 2: Design & Testing (2 months)

  • Redesigned core workflows with input from end users
  • Created process documentation and standard operating procedures
  • Piloted new processes with 2 teams to gather feedback
  • Iterated based on real-world usage and pain points

Phase 3: Implementation & Training (2 months)

  • Rolled out new processes company-wide in waves
  • Conducted training sessions for all employees
  • Established metrics dashboard for ongoing monitoring
  • Created feedback loops for continuous improvement

Redesigned workflow showing streamlined handoffs between teams

Key Initiatives

Automated Data Pipeline: Implemented automated data collection and reporting, eliminating 180+ hours of manual work per month across the organization.

Cross-Functional Playbooks: Created standardized playbooks for common cross-team workflows, reducing miscommunication and rework by 40%.

Metrics Dashboard: Built a real-time operational dashboard giving leadership visibility into key metrics and enabling data-driven decision making.

Results & Impact

The initiative delivered substantial improvements across multiple dimensions:

  • 30% efficiency gain in core operational workflows
  • 180+ hours saved monthly through automation
  • 40% reduction in cross-team miscommunication
  • 95% employee satisfaction with new processes (up from 62%)
  • 2-day reduction in average time-to-delivery for key outputs

Lessons Learned

Change management is critical: The technical solution was only 30% of the work. The other 70% was helping people adapt to new ways of working through training, communication, and support.

Pilot programs work: Testing with small groups first allowed us to identify and fix issues before full rollout, saving significant time and frustration.

Document everything: Creating clear documentation and SOPs ensured sustainability and made it easier to onboard new team members.
